Welcome to our Merry!

Backed with a solid recipe to satisfy your palate with the most authentic platters, robust flavors, premium ingredients, and loads of creative meals – Rigid Foods was born. This global brand is every foodie’s haven of meat-based delights, and the most highlighted factor is the brand’s farm fresh, succulent, freshly prepared juicy burgers. With properly trained chefs, customer service teams, innovative recipes, and affordable meals, Juigo has become an instant hit among both local and international food lovers.
